To change screen resolution to 1024 x 768 in Ubuntu:
1. On the left panel, click the System Settings icon.
The System Settings window is displayed.
2. Click the Displays icon.
The Displays dialog box is displayed.
3. From the Resolution drop-down list, select 1024 x 768.
4. Click the Apply button.
A confirmation message is displayed.
5. Click Keep This Configuration.
